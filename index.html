Welcome to Belgian Shadow Canes

My late father Edy VEECKMANS

13/07/1939 - 05/06-2021

It took me three years (2019-2021) to master the technique because I wanted to make a special cane for my father, unfortunately he was not allowed to experience this because I worked on his cane in secret, but only two months before I really succeeded, he passed away.

Now this Cane will be passed on from generation to generation so that they can view their grandfather's great-grandfather's shadow and tell the story behind the cane to their children.
